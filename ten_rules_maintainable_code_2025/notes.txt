10 Tips to Keep Your Software Simple:
1. YAGNI - You ain't gonna need it
2. DRY - Don't repeat yourself
3. Avoid overengineering
4. Organize yourself
5. Keep functions short
6. Avoid hardcoded values
7. Use meaningful names
8. Document your thought process, not just what the code does
9. Don't overcomplicate you module structure
10. Write tests for critical code
Bonus. Spend 10% of your time on refactoring
